About
Words offer the means to meaning, the very medium that facilitates the flow of thoughts stands underrated for the masses but here at Alfaaz ADGITM we believe in enunciating the truth with panache. Alfaaz aims to be a haven to all the literary artists who set foot in the hallowed halls of ADGITM. We offer you a canvas free from the shackles of reality, one you can paint with the colours of your own imagination. Here at Alfaaz we encourage you to fulfil your creative potential in the form of poems and prose, without the restrictions of language. We invite all Hindi kavis, urdu shayars and english writers to come aboard and grace the stage with their crafts. We also serve to be the funny bone of college by incorporating stand up comics and humorous poets. Explore an avenue bejewelled by creativity and content by joining this collective of literary free birds.
